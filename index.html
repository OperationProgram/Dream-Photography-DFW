About Dream Photography DFW

Hey, I'm Noell S. - a passionate photographer from Dallas, Texas, with over 19 years of experience capturing special moments at weddings and events. I'm all about the country culture and love working with families to create lasting memories. I studied photography at Richland College and, beyond photography, I've been a hairstylist for 33 years in the Lakewood area. When not behind the lens or salon chair, I enjoy gardening, painting furniture, and hanging out with my two beautiful Akitas. Can't wait to create fantastic memories with you! Let's have fun and capture moments that last a lifetime.
